Handling of Yard Waste

Spring is on its way!

With that said, yard waste and tree trimmings should be composted or placed at the curb for pick up, not dumped on CMIA Community Property. You may think it is only leaves, but it builds up into debris and becomes trash.

We have been working hard to beautify our neighborhood. Anything dumped on East Drive, Severn Rd, Park Rd, and the woods at Hillbottom can become our responsibility to cleanup. The community has had to deal with, and sometimes pay for, the removal of leaves, yard trash, and tree branches on community property or in the woods.
